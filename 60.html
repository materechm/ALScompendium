Title: Astrocytes from familial and sporadic ALS patients are toxic to motor neurons

Year Published: 2011

Observation: co-cultured MNs with sALS astrocytes. After 120h there were 45-70% fewer MNs compared to non ALS controls

Comment: Identify astrocytes as a non cell autonomous component in sALS
