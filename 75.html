Title: FALS with FUS mutation in Japan, with early onset, rapid progress and basophilic inclusion

Year Published: 2010

Observation: Detected a missense mutation in FUS gene that substituted cysteine for arginine at residue 512 and other 4 mutations in exon 15 of FUS

Comment: Identified fALS with FUS mutations in Japan

Citation: Suzuki, N., Aoki, M., Warita, H., Kato, M., Mizuno, H., Shimakura, N., … Itoyama, Y. (2010). FALS with FUS mutation in Japan, with early onset, rapid progress and basophilic inclusion. Journal of Human Genetics, 55(4), 252–4. doi:10.1038/jhg.2010.16
