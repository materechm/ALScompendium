Title: Position-dependent FUS-RNA interactions regulate alternative splicing events and transcriptions

Year Published: 2012

Observation: KO FUS and found that FUS is likely to exert neuron-specific gene regulation by modulating alternative splicing events. FUS is bound to the antisense RNA strand at the promoter regions, which downregulated transcription of the coding strand

Comment: FUS regulates alternative splicing events and transcriptions in a position-dependent manner
