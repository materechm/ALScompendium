Title: Ligand binding and aggregation of pathogenic SOD1

Year Published: 2013

Observation: Structural perturbation of A4V SOD1 monomer, which readily aggregates. High isoproterenol concentrations may promote SOD1 oligonerization

Comment: Intervention to stabilize SOD1 dimer and inhibit aggregation is regarded as potential therapeutic strategy
