Title: A role for motoneuron subtype-selective ER stress in disease manifestations of FALS mice

Year Published: 2009

Observation: Comparable ER stress preceding pathology in VUL and RES motoneurons. VUL MNs are selectively prone to ER stress. Reducing ER stress aleviated disease manifestations

Comment: ER stress responses in specific motoneuron subtypes influence the progressive manifestations of weakening and paralysis
