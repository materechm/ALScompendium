Title: The potential of GPNMB as novel neuroprotective factor in amyotrophic lateral sclerosis

Year Published: 2012

Observation: Downregulation of GPNMB increased motor neuron vulnerability. Fragments of GPNMB in spinal cord attenuated the neurotoxicity of SOD1 G93A mice

Comment: GPNMB can be a target for therapeutic intervention in ALS
